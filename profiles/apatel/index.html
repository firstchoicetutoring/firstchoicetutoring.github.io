About Me

Hi everyone! I’m Azita Patel, a tutor here at First Choice Tutoring. I’m currently a student at Folsom Lake College, with plans of transferring to a 4-year university to pursue a degree in Biomedical Engineering due to my love for math and science. I have been tutoring since my sophomore year in high school, and am currently finishing my sophomore year in college, and hope to tutor for many more years as sharing knowledge is the best joy. I’ve always been goal oriented, and all about breaking barriers, whether those barriers be mental, emotional or physical barriers. A major key for success is the will power to learn- if you really try, you’ll realize you learn something new everyday!
